## Who this is for

Anyone creating a new Outpay merchant account for the first time.

## Before you begin

- Your store's name and, optionally, a short description.
- An EVM wallet you control on Base, and the ability to sign a message with it (either a browser wallet extension, or a manually pasted signature).

## Create your store

<Steps>
<Step>Open the Outpay Dashboard and create your account.</Step>
<Step>Enter the store name and optional description.</Step>
<Step>Enter the EVM wallet address you control for Base payments.</Step>
<Step>Sign the wallet-ownership challenge. Outpay validates the address format, its checksum, the signature, and the timestamp before accepting it.</Step>
<Step>Review the store details and finish onboarding.</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="error" title="Verify before accepting payments">
Confirm the wallet address character-by-character against your treasury records. Customers send funds directly to this address — a wrong wallet is not something Outpay can delay or reverse.
</Callout>

## Expected result

Your store is created, and your payout wallet is registered and verified. You can now create checkouts and payment links from the Dashboard.

## Common problems

| Problem | Likely cause | Next step |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wallet signature is rejected | The signature was signed more than a few minutes after the challenge was issued, or with the wrong wallet. | Regenerate the challenge and sign it promptly with the exact wallet you entered. |
| Onboarding cannot be completed | Required store or wallet fields are missing or invalid. | Confirm the wallet address matches the required format and try again. |

You can update the payout wallet later from **Settings**. That path requires a fresh ownership signature and should be treated as a high-impact access event — see [payout wallet](/docs/merchants/payout-wallet).
