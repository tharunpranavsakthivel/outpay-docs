Settlement is direct, wallet-to-wallet:

1. The merchant registers a payout wallet and proves ownership of it.
2. Outpay embeds that recipient address in every new checkout.
3. The customer sends USDC on Base to the recipient address.
4. Outpay's payment matcher verifies the transfer and notifies the merchant.

Outpay does not custody customer funds, hold a merchant balance, sweep funds, or execute a payout transaction anywhere in this flow.

<Callout type="warning" title="No refund API">
Refunds are not implemented. If a customer sends an incorrect amount, or pays after a checkout has expired, preserve the transaction evidence and use an operator-approved manual remediation process — do not represent a manual wallet transfer as an Outpay product feature.
</Callout>
